How to Play

  • Build foundation piles Ace to King per suit (top-right).
  • Tableau: descending rank, alternating colors.
  • Only a King can fill an empty tableau column.
  • Click the deck to draw 1 card at a time.
  • Click the top drawn card again to send it back into the deck.
  • Drag cards or click to select, then click a destination.
  • Face-down cards flip automatically when exposed.
